Our Vision

To promote a secure environment for children in a friendly, homely atmosphere

To demonstrate the recognition and value of play in our children’s learning and development

To acknowledge that each child is a unique individual who is a competent and capable learner.

To foster the social, Emotional, Physical, Cognitive and Language development recognising different learning styles, and the needs of the individual child.

To provide staff that are nurturing, experienced, qualified and professional.
